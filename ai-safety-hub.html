Learning path

Check AI answers before they become decisions.

This path is for readers who already use AI but want a better review habit. It covers hallucinations, privacy, citations, source quality, risk levels, and when a human expert or official source needs to step in.

Best for Careful AI users

Estimated path time: 45 to 65 minutes plus the output checker resource.

Rate the risk

Safety rule

The higher the consequence, the stronger the review.

AI output does not need the same review for every task. A brainstorming draft can be checked lightly. A claim about health, money, legal rights, safety, hiring, education, or customer trust needs stronger verification.

This path teaches a simple habit: identify the risk, protect private information, check the source, and keep a person responsible for the final decision.

Extra review required

  • Medical, legal, financial, safety, or hiring advice.
  • AI claims with citations that have not been opened directly.
  • Output based on private customer, student, employee, or family data.
  • Content that will be published publicly or sent to customers.
