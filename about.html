About

The AI Explainer explains AI in plain English.

The AI Explainer is a small publication concept for readers who want practical, easy-to-understand explanations of artificial intelligence, AI tools, privacy risks, and everyday use cases.

The site is designed to publish original guides and summaries written for non-technical readers. Each article should focus on clear examples, responsible use, and source-aware explanations.
