About

The AI Explainer is a small publication about artificial intelligence and practical judgment.

The site publishes careful essays for readers who want to use AI tools without losing context, privacy, authorship, or responsibility. The focus is practical: how AI changes writing, research, work habits, decision-making, and everyday digital life.

The AI Explainer avoids model hype and empty productivity claims. Each article is written to be useful on its own, with clear limits around sensitive topics and a preference for plain language over jargon.

Editorial standards

  • Articles separate practical advice from speculation.
  • Claims about tools, policy, safety, or public facts are reviewed before publication.
  • High-stakes topics include limits and encourage qualified review where appropriate.
  • Published pages are checked for broken links, unclear phrasing, and outdated assumptions.
