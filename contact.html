Contact and Corrections Policy

The AI Explainer is an informational website. It does not provide private AI consulting, personal troubleshooting, sponsored placement requests, or one-to-one message support.

Site-related notices may be reviewed when they help keep the publication accurate, lawful, and safe. A personal reply is not guaranteed.

  • Article corrections: include the article title and the exact sentence that may need review.
  • Copyright or removal notices: identify the page URL and the material in question.
  • Privacy, advertising, or security concerns: describe the issue without sharing sensitive details.

Please do not send passwords, account details, medical information, legal documents, financial records, or other confidential personal information.
