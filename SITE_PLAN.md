# The AI Explainer Website Plan

## Goal

Build a small English AI explainer site that can later apply for Google AdSense. The site should look like a helpful publication first and an advertising site second.

## Audience

Everyday readers who want simple explanations of AI tools, AI concepts, and safe practical use.

## Content Pillars

1. AI Basics
   - What is artificial intelligence?
   - What is generative AI?
   - What is a prompt?
   - What is machine learning?

2. AI Tools
   - ChatGPT vs Gemini vs Claude
   - AI image generators explained
   - Microsoft Copilot for normal users
   - Canva AI and design workflows

3. Work and Study
   - How to use AI to write better emails
   - How to summarize documents with AI
   - How students can use AI responsibly
   - How to check AI answers before using them

4. Risks and Responsible Use
   - What not to share with AI tools
   - AI hallucinations explained
   - Copyright basics for AI-generated content
   - Bias and misinformation in AI tools

## Ad Placement Strategy

- Use one leaderboard ad near the top after approval.
- Use one in-article ad after the opening explanation.
- Use one sidebar display ad on desktop.
- Avoid asking users to click ads.
- Avoid placing ads so close to buttons or navigation that users may click accidentally.

## Pages Needed Before AdSense

- Home
- Article pages
- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Copyright or DMCA policy

## One-Day Test Scope

- Publish the landing page design.
- Add 8 to 10 starter articles.
- Add placeholder compliance pages.
- Connect Google Search Console after deployment.
- Apply for AdSense only after the site has enough original content.
